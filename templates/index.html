Our Story

A classic café with a modern soul

Café Aroma is a Greek family-owned neighborhood treasure at {{ info.address }}. From the moment you walk in, you feel the warmth — the kind that comes from genuine passion for good coffee and great hospitality.

Owner Paul and his family have built something rare: a place where locals feel at home and visitors feel they've found a secret. Coffee is at the heart of everything — from silky Lavazza espresso to the iconic Freddo that keeps everyone coming back.
